This application presents comprehensive findings from an in-depth analysis of weather metrics in Boston collected from 2013 to 2023.
It offers diverse analysis parameters that meticulously investigate, analyse, evaluate and visualise different points of interest essential for city planners, environmentalists, and the general public.

These insights are crucial for understanding long-term climatic patterns, assessing seasonal changes, and anticipating future weather conditions.

Key functionalities include:

Interactive Visualizations: Users can interact with dynamic charts and graphs that illustrate various weather parameters, making complex data more accessible, understandable, and insightful.

Bespoke Queries: Tailor your data exploration by selecting specific years, seasons, or weather phenomena to focus on particular aspects of the data, allowing for targeted analysis.

Educational Insights: The platform serves as an educational tool that elucidates the factors driving weather changes in Boston.
